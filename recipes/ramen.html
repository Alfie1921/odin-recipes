Beef Miso Ramen

The Recipe

This type of Ramen in a miso broth is packed full of delicious flavours, missing japan? this is just the meal you need

Ingredients

  • 8 oz sirloin beef steak
  • 1/2 cup tamari sauce
  • 1 tsp coconut oil
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tsp miso paste
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 6 oz ramen noodles

Directons

  1. marinade steak in tamari sauce for 2 hours and remove excess marinade afterwards
  2. medium-high heat skillet with coconut oil, add steak and cook to preference. allow to rest for 1o minutes
  3. whilst the steak rests combine: broth, miso paste, garlic, and sesame oil over medium heat.
  4. once at a slow boil, add the ramen noodles cooking until soft (approx 3 minutes).
  5. finally season with salt and pepper (and other preferences)
