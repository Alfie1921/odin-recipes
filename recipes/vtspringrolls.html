Vietnamese Fresh Spring Rolls

The Recipe

Veitnamese Spring Rolls are an amazing lighter variation of traditional fried spring rolls, perfect for hot days and decilious dipped in sauce!

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces rice vermicelli
  • 8 rice wrappers
  • 8 large cooked shrimp, prepared
  • 2 lettuce leaves, chopped
  • 3 tbsp chopped fresh mint
  • 3 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
  • 4/3 tbst chopped fresh thai basil

Sauces

  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 4 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp garlic chili sauce
  • 3 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp finely chopped peanuts

Directions

  1. Add vermicelli to a pot of boiling salted water for 3-5 minutes of until it is tender but firm to bite
  2. Dip a rice wrapper in warm water for a second to soften
  3. Inside the wrapper lay 2 shrimp halves, vermicelli, lettuce, mint, cilantro, and basil. leave space on all sices
  4. Fold uncovered sides inward and tighly roll the wrapper so no side is left open
  5. Repeat steps 3-4 for all remaining ingredients until you have made 8 spring rolls
  6. Sauce One: mix the water, lime juice, sugar, fish sauce, garlic, and chilli sauce until combined
  7. Sauce Two: mix the hoisin sauce and peanuts in a separate bowl

Enjoy!
