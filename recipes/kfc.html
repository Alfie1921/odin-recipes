Korean Fried Chicken

The Recipe

Looking for restaurant worthy fried chicken? it doesn't get much better than this. the perfect combination of plate-scratching crispiness and a mouth-watering tenderness

Ingredients

The Marinade

  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken thighs, quartered
  • 1/2 yellow onion, grated
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

The Batter

  • 3/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup self raising flower
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup cold water

Directions

  1. make marinade: stir together chickenm onion, garic, salt, and pepper in a suitable bowl until chicken is coated. cover and refrigerate 4 hours or overnight.
  2. heat oil in deep fryer or sarge saucepan to 170 degrees C or 340 degrees F.
  3. make batter: whisk together cornstarch, flour, sugar, pepper, and salt in a suitable bowl.
  4. gradually whisk in cold water until mixture resembles pancake batter, remove chicken and cover in batter, discarding remaining marinade.
  5. fry chicken in hot oil for 4 minutes and place on cooling rack.
  6. increase oil temperature to 190 degrees C or 375 degrees F.
  7. fry chicken again in hot oil until golden brown and crispy. (~3-4 minutes)
  8. allow to cool, apply favourite sauces, and enjoy!
